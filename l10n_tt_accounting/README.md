# Trinidad & Tobago Accounting Localization

Odoo Community + Odoo Mates Accounting localization starter for Trinidad & Tobago.

## v1.3.0

Adds a visible **Trinidad & Tobago** settings section under Odoo Settings so the setup can be applied directly from Settings.

After upgrading the module:

1. Restart Odoo.
2. Apps > Update Apps List.
3. Upgrade **Trinidad & Tobago Accounting Localization**.
4. Go to **Settings > Trinidad & Tobago**.
5. Click **Apply / Refresh Trinidad Setup**.
6. Click **Open Bank Setup** to add local banks and create matching journals/accounts.

The setup configures Trinidad & Tobago country, TTD currency, 12.5% VAT defaults, Trinidad-oriented accounts, journals, fiscal positions, and bank journals.
